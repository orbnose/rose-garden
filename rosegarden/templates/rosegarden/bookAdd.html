What should I use as the Dewey Decimal Number?

You should be able to find the most common Dewey Decimal number for non-fiction books by searching for the book at classify.oclc.org/classify2/
(Search by ISBN or title/author).

Fiction, literature, and poetry can be logged under 800.
Biographies use 921.

When in doubt, choose the most relevant category and number below:
Dewey Decimal Number Category
000Computer science, information & general works
100Philosophy & psychology
200Religion
300Social sciences
400Language
500Science
600Technology
700Arts & recreation
800Literature
900History & geography
